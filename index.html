Safety Protocols

In Hiking and Trekking

Make sure to pack up enough water

Make sure to pack up enough water before going hiking or trekking. It's easy to underestimate how much you'll need for the trip so bring extra water just to be safe to prevent dehydration.

Preventing frostbite from taking over

Be careful not to get too cold. Make sure to keep warm and stay at the right temperature. In the case of that emergency make sure to bring warm clothing to stay heated to prevent that problem from happening in the first place.

Staying chill and resisting the heat

The heat can be quite intense. Make sure to pack up some sunscreens to prevent sunburns while you're hiking or trekking while also trying to stay in areas where the heat doesn't affect you too much.

Bring proper equipment and plentiful supplies

Make sure to bring enough supplies such as food, water, and extra clothing for lengthy hiking or trekking trips. It's always good to stock up a bit more than required to be safe and for cases of emergency.
